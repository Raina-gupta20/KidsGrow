ABOUT US

KidsGrow is a charitable organization dedicated to empowering underprivileged children through education, healthcare, and nutrition. We provide school supplies, scholarships, tutoring, balanced meals, and medical assistance to create a safe and nurturing environment for children to learn and grow.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common queries about KidsGrow.

How can I donate?
You can donate online via our website using secure payment methods like credit/debit cards, PayPal, or direct bank transfer.
Where does my money go?
Your donations are used for education, healthcare, food, and shelter for underprivileged children. Every penny is accounted for transparently.
How can I volunteer?
You can sign up as a volunteer through our website. We have various roles, including teaching, organizing events, and fundraising.
Is my donation tax-deductible?
Yes, all donations to KidsGrow are tax-deductible. You will receive a receipt for tax purposes.
How do I contact KidsGrow?
You can reach us via email at support@kidsgrow.org or call our helpline at +91 7987493470.
